Our Mission

The team at VIZE subscribes to the mission that all college applicants should have access to the same guides, tips, and strategies, regardless of resources. That's why the VIZE app exists: to provide both student-and school-specific guidance throughout every step of your process.

Some Of Our Services

Experienced Advice

VIZE's team of experienced college counselors will help you along every step of the college application process, from the initial college search to presenting your final application in a way that highlights the best in you and nothing else. Every word of guidance we offer comes from over a decade of experience in the college advising industry, so you won't have to.

Data-Driven Approach

VIZE leverages the most recent college data to ensure that you have the best chances possible at all of your prospective colleges and universities. We'll help you understand which activities and statistics are most important for each individual school on your list, and provide you with guidance to reach all of their necessary benchmarks.

Personalization

College ranking systems each construct their own algorithm for deciding which schools are best, but we doubt they ever consulted you before doing so. VIZE will help you create your own ranking system based on a series of conversational questionnaires that allow you to tell us what's important to you. The right place for you is out there, and we'll help you find it.

Customizable

VIZE understands that each member of our community has unique needs surrounding the college application process, and we've developed an interface that allows everyone to find what they're looking for. Users are free to customize their own college lists or enlist our help, receive automated reminders of each necessary task along the way or create their own, and immerse themselves within a social media community to ask questions and stay motivated along the way.
